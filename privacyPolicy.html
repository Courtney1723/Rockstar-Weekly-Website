Privacy Policy

• Data storage allows the autopost command to function properly.

• If you do not subscribe to auto posts and do not configure auto post settings no data is stored.

• The first time you click any button on the autopost command your server ID will be stored and the default settings will be applied.

• If you subsribe to auto posts the channel ID of whatever channel you chose will be stored.

• If you add a role that can configure auto posts the role ID of whatever role you chose will be stored.

• Your server ID will remain stored as long as the Rockstar Weekly bot remains a member of your server.

• Channel IDs will be stored until you remove the channel using the /autopost command or kick the bot from the server.

• Role IDs will be stored until you remove the role using the /autopost command or kick the bot from the server.

• This bot was developed and is maintained by Courtney1723#1521 who is located in Colorado, USA.

• I am the only person who will view your data and it is always completely anonymous.

• You can contact me by email or by joining the Rockstar Weekly support server.

• You can remove all stored data by kicking the Rockstar Weekly bot from a server.

• You can remove channel ID data by typing /autopost, clicking 'Stop', clicking 'GTA' or 'RDO', and then selecting the channel you want to remove.

• You can remove role ID data by typing /autopost, clicking 'configure', clicking 'remove', and selecting the role you want to remove.

• You can manually request your data to be removed by joining the Rockstar Weekly support server.
